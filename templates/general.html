Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver an exceptional flying experience that exceeds passenger expectations. By focusing on customer service, safety, and innovation, we aim to be the most preferred airline in the region.

  • In-flight Entertainment: Enjoy a wide selection of movies, TV shows, music, and games to keep you entertained throughout your flight.

Rules and Regulations

Booking and Ticketing

To make your booking experience easy, we offer an online ticketing platform where you can search flights, select your preferred dates, and make payments securely. Our booking system is designed to provide quick and efficient services, and your tickets will be emailed to you once the payment is confirmed.

  • Booking Window: Flights can be booked from 6 months to 24 hours before departure.
  • Payment Policy: We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and online payment options like UPI, net banking, and wallets.
  • Ticket Amendments: Changes to tickets can be made, but an amendment fee may apply depending on the type of ticket purchased.

Cancellation and Refund Policy

If you need to cancel your flight, you can do so by visiting our website or contacting our customer service. Cancellation requests must be made at least 12 hours before the scheduled departure time. Depending on the type of ticket and the time of cancellation, the following refund rules apply:

  • Standard Ticket: Full refund if canceled 24 hours before the flight. A partial refund may apply if canceled later.
  • Non-refundable Ticket: No refund will be provided for cancellations.
  • Refund Processing: Refunds may take up to 7-10 business days to process, depending on the payment method.

Baggage Allowance

Each passenger is entitled to carry a certain amount of baggage as part of their flight ticket. Please ensure that you adhere to the following baggage regulations:

  • Checked Baggage: 1 piece of checked baggage up to 20kg is allowed for economy class passengers. Business class passengers are allowed up to 30kg.
  • Carry-on Baggage: 1 piece of carry-on baggage up to 7kg is allowed. Ensure your carry-on fits within the specified dimensions of 55cm x 40cm x 20cm.
  • Excess Baggage: Charges for excess baggage will be applied at the rate specified on our website.

Passenger Rights

At India Airways, we value your rights as a passenger. We adhere to all local and international regulations to ensure your safety and comfort. Some of the key passenger rights include:

  • On-Time Performance: We strive to ensure that flights depart and arrive on time. If there is any delay, passengers will be informed in advance and provided with compensation as per our policies.
  • Refunds for Canceled Flights: In the event of a flight cancellation, passengers are entitled to a full refund or rebooking on the next available flight.
  • Compensation for Overbooked Flights: If a flight is overbooked, passengers who are denied boarding will be compensated with travel vouchers, rebooking on the next flight, and other benefits.

Health and Safety Regulations

Your safety and well-being are our top priorities. We strictly follow all health and safety regulations issued by aviation authorities and health organizations. Please note the following guidelines:

  • COVID-19 Protocol: Passengers are required to wear masks throughout the flight and may be asked to provide proof of vaccination or negative test results, depending on the travel destination.
  • In-Flight Safety: All passengers must follow the in-flight safety instructions, including fastening seatbelts during takeoff and landing, and not carrying prohibited items.
  • Assistance for Special Needs: We offer special assistance for passengers with disabilities or medical conditions. Please inform us 48 hours in advance if you need assistance during the flight.
